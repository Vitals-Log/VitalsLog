Guide

Getting Started

1

Download & Open

Download Vitals Logbook from the App Store. Open the app and complete the brief onboarding to set up your profile.

2

Add Your First Entry

Tap the "+" tab to add an entry. Enter your vitals like blood pressure and heart rate. Add notes if desired.

3

View Your Data

Check the Today tab for a snapshot, Log tab for history, and Trends tab for charts showing patterns over time.

4

Set Reminders

Go to Settings and enable daily reminders so you never forget to log your vitals consistently.
